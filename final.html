SOVIET MISSILE DODGE


This is my collaborative final project for CSC 106.

This project is a simple side-scrolling bullet hell game in which you take control of a small biplane and attempt
to dodge comically oversized munitions headed your way! Use the space bar to keep the plane in the air,
if you hit the ground or suffer 3 hits by a missile, you're dead!


THE STORY


It is February 1949. The German Reich won the Second World War, and the Soviet Union has fallen far, with the Germans
taking territory up to Moscow and the Japanese taking territory in Siberia. There seems to be little hope left for the state to continue functioning.

However, recent events (mainly the death of The Fuhrer himself) have given Soviet resistance members a chance to
band together and strike back at their oppressors. You are a spy pilot for the resistance and have just finished a mission stealing
blueprints from the German Reich that is planning to create a nuclear weapon. You must bring these blueprints to headquarters,
but as you take off, you are assaulted by newly-operational German missile systems that span miles in the snow.

Can you survive the onslaught of hundreds of missiles and bring the plans back to the Motherland?
